Work with us

Fragaria is a company with some serious software development history. The company is built upon people with real skills. You can be sure to learn a lot. Fast. There is a lot of learning opportunities for you.

Besides developing great software, we like being people too. That's why we value friendship and understanding as our top priorities.

We treat employees like people, not like machines. Expect a great team full of interesting characters (beers and parties included).

Working agile

No waterfalls here. We belive agile is the right way to do it.

Open source

Home office

Pet friendly
